Participation in University CADE 2022

On behalf of the South Headquarters of Tecusp, my person was selected to participate in this event of important leaders at the national level, with a location in Lima.

After a selection process through a conversation in which superior educational quality was discussed, I was chosen along with a colleague from another specialty to represent our headquarters at the event.

In this 100% face-to-face edition, the aim is to convey that it is time to unite, recognize our diversity, dialogue and take action in search of the common good. The country needs us!

The days on which the event will take place are June 29, 30 and July 1, at the Peruvian naval base located in Callao.

CADE Universitario is the most important and diverse meeting of young leaders in the country, which aims to generate a space for reflection, inspiration and action for two and a half days, whose purpose is to encourage young people to take action to contribute to the development of the country, improving their understanding of reality and connecting them with each other and with leaders of other generations.
